This guide will show you how to migrate a site that meets the following criteria to Drupal 9:

|Criteria|Value
|---|---
|Pantheon Hosted| Yes
|Drupal Version| 8
|Multi-Dev Environment | No
|Build Tools| No
|Composer Managed|No
|Custom Upstream| No
